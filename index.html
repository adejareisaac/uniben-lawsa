OUR
RESPONSIBILITY

The Law Students' Association exists to serve, protect, and empower every law student at the University of Benin. Our functions span academic support, welfare advocacy, social organization, and professional development.

01
Academic Support & Resources

We curate and distribute past examination questions, textbooks, lecture notes, and legal reference materials across all levels — 100L through 500L — ensuring every student has access to the academic tools they need to excel. Our resource library is updated each semester by the Executive team.

02
Student Welfare & Advocacy

The welfare of law students is our highest priority. From academic grievances and examination irregularities to campus safety and faculty-student relations, LAWSA stands as the first line of defence for every member. We engage the Faculty of Law and University management through structured dialogue and representation.

03
Social Events & Programmes

Beyond the lecture hall, LAWSA organizes Law Week, annual dinners, inter-faculty debates, legal aid clinics, and social mixers that build lasting bonds among law students. We recognize that the social environment of a law student is as critical as their academic performance.

04
Professional Development

We bridge the gap between legal education and professional practice through seminars, law clinic partnerships, moot court coaching, and mentorship programmes connecting students with practising lawyers, judges, and legal professionals across Nigeria.

It is with great pleasure and a profound sense of pride that I welcome you to the official website of the Law Students Association (LAWSA), University of Benin. This platform has been designed to serve as a central space where Lawsans, alumni, partners, and visitors can access important information about our Association, stay updated on our activities, explore opportunities, and connect with the various initiatives that continue to shape our community.
